About Wishly

Wishly is a simple online greeting platform created to help people share personalized messages with ease. Our goal is to make digital greetings more meaningful by allowing users to create custom greeting links using names.

In a world where messages are often rushed and generic, Wishly provides a quick and thoughtful way to connect with friends, family, and loved ones during special moments such as holidays, birthdays, and celebrations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make online greetings personal, simple, and accessible to everyone. We believe small personalized gestures can create meaningful connections.

Why Wishly Exists

Wishly was created to remove the stress of designing greeting cards or writing messages from scratch. With just a name and a click, users can generate a shareable greeting link in seconds.

Transparency

Wishly does not require user registration and does not store personal data entered into the greeting generator. We are committed to providing a safe, user-friendly, and transparent experience.
